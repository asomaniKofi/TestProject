The impressive Grandhotel Pupp is located in the historical centre of Karlovy Vary. It offers spacious rooms, onsite restaurants and exclusive wellness centre with a relaxation pool, meditation bath, sauna and steam bath, as well as a fitness room. A wide range of beauty and wellness treatments including a salt cave, physiotherapy and massages is available.

All rooms feature a satellite TV, some of them come with a balcony.
Digital newspaper service, as well as bike rental are available. During the winter season, guests may enjoy special discounts in a ski resort located 19.9 miles from the hotel.

Located in the heart of Karlovy Vary, this luxury hotel is within a 5-minute walk of Diana Lookout Tower and Karlovy Vary Municiple Theater. Church of St. Mary Magdalene and Mill Colonnade are also within 10 minutes.
Along with 2 restaurants, this hotel has a full-service spa and a casino. WiFi in public areas is free. Additionally, an indoor pool, a nightclub and 2 bars/lounges are on-site.
All 228 individually decorated properties feature free WiFi and minibars. Thoughtful touches include bathrobes and slippers, and guests will also find room service and televisions.
Bohemian specialities and international dishes are served in the various onsite restaurants. Traditional cakes and desserts can be enjoyed at Café Pupp and on a summer terrace. Little Hall, an onsite restaurant and Becher´s Bar invite you to enjoy the evening entertainment, including a live band. .
